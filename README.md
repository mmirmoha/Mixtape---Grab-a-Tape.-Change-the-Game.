# 🎵 Mixtape

**Grab a tape. Change the game.**

Mixtape is an arcade endless runner with a twist: it keeps changing what *kind* of game it is. You play as **Bit**, a tiny music sprite trapped inside an old cassette tape, auto-running through a song to escape. Grab a glowing cassette and the whole game instantly transforms — your movement, the hazards, and the look all flip to a different mode. Stay calm and adapt as the rules change underneath you.

Built in **Unity 6**

## 🎮 The Four Tracks

Every cassette you grab swaps you into one of four modes:

- **Side-Scroll** — classic platformer; jump over gaps, spikes, and blocks.
- **Gravity-Flip** — tap to invert gravity and run along the ceiling or floor.
- **Top-Down** — overhead view; strafe to dodge obstacles and graze them for combo.
- **Rhythm-Tap** — ride the beat and tap notes in time on the hit line.

## 🕹️ Controls

| Action | Keyboard | Touch / Mouse |
|---|---|---|
| Jump / Flip / Tap-on-beat | `Space` *(rebindable)* | Click / Tap |
| Strafe (Top-Down) | `W` / `S` / Arrow keys | — |
| Confirm / Back | `Enter` / `Esc` | Tap buttons |

One hit ends the run — survive as long as you can and chase a high score.

## ✨ Features

- Four genre-mashing modes that swap mid-run, with a flashy "fast-forward" transition.
- **Combo multiplier** that rewards clean play (rhythm hits, flip-chains, near-misses).
- First-run **tutorial** that teaches the controls.
- **Options**: volume sliders, reduced-flashing mode, colorblind palette, difficulty, and key rebinding.
- **Shop**: spend coins earned in runs on cosmetic Bit skins, palettes, and an extra music track.
